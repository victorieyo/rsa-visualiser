About Me

Hi, I'm Thanh Cong

I'm a passionate developer with a strong interest in computer science. I created this RSA Visualiser Project as a way to help people and myself understand the RSA encryption process and its underlying mathematics.

My Journey

Developing a passion for mathematics, I was inspired by my math teacher, who always emphasized the importance of drafting out thoughts and processes to gain a clearer understanding of the problem at hand. This approach has shaped my love for visualizations in algorithms, as I believe they help break down complex concepts into something more comprehensible.

Why RSA Visualizer?

The goal of this project is to provide an interactive way to explore how the RSA algorithm works. The RSA Visualizer lets you see key steps, from key generation to encryption and decryption, which can help students, developers, and cryptography enthusiasts understand the complexities of RSA.
